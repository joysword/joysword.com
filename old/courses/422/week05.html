In-Class Review of Project 1


This week we will do some in-class work related to project 1
Tuesday - Break into eight groups, compare what you did in Project 1 and create a new consensus solution in your group.

By Wednesday at 9pm your group should create a public web page that describes your consensus design for a better user interface. The web page should include a drawing of the final design and your rationale for why this is a good design. The webpage should include the names of all your team members. Please email the location of this webpage to andy and include in the email an image of your overall interface so we can share off of those with the class on Thursday.

Thursday - Each of the eight groups will present their solution to the class. Each group will have 6 minutes to present their design and their rationale for it.
