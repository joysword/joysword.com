My name is Shi Yin. I'm a PhD student in Computer Science at the University of Illinois at Chicago.
My re­search interests are Computer Graphics, Visualization, Virtual Reality and Human-Computer Interaction.
I received my Bachelor's Degree in Information and Computation Science at University of Electronic Science and Technology of China in 2011.
I'm a typical Sagittarius. I like sports, movies, video games and everything good in my life.
I have quite a large hard disk but my memory and register are not good enough. All my current work is to: 1) fill in more into my hard disk; 2) organize useful information in better memory and register rather than in the out-dated hard disk.

殷实（1988-），字履诚，又字思诚（语出《孟子》：“诚者，天之道也；思诚者，人之道也”）。徐州广陵（今江苏扬州）人，典型射手座。文效诸葛，武仿岳飞，与其两位发小合称为“三大愣种”。民国九十六年毕业于江苏省扬州中学。同年入蜀，求学于清水河畔，时年18岁。时值国家内忧不绝外患不断，立誓尽忠为民，鞠躬尽瘁。民国一百年（2011年）远赴美利坚求学，心系天下，志存高远，以弘扬大汉文化，振兴华夏文明为己任。在灌水界享有崇高地位。又好观赏电影，以拖进度条为耻，还好电子游戏，正宗伪职业玩家，虚拟世界受虐狂。
现正力争成为伟大的骨灰业余玩家、哲学博士和爱国企业家。
