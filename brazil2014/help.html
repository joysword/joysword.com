帮助

只要你的应用支持iCalendar，你就可以使用我们的日历。如果你需要帮助，参见下方教程。如果你使用的日历应用没有列出来，请参考你的应用的教程或者联系我。

  • Mac OS X Calendar/iCal
  • Apple iPhone/iPad/iPod Touch
  • Microsoft Outlook
  • Windows Live Calendar
  • Google Calendar

救命！我的日历没有自动更新！

Mac OS X Calendar/iCal

点击首页上的‘订阅到桌面/移动客户端日历’即可。如果不行，参考以下步骤:


  1. Open the 'Calendar' application, or the 'iCal' application on older versions of Mac OS X.
  2. Click on 'File', then 'New Calendar Subscription...'.
  3. Enter the calendar URL (http://joysword.com/brazil2014/cal/all.ics?noCache by default) and click 'Subscribe'.
  4. Review the information and click 'OK', we recommend changing 'Auto-refresh' to 'Every day' to ensure the calendar is up-to-date.
  5. Your calendar should now be added to your Mac.

Apple iPhone/iPad/iPod Touch

You should be able to subscribe by tapping 'Subscribe in Desktop / Mobile Calendar' on our homepage. If not, please follow the instructions below.


  1. Open the 'Settings' app.
  2. Tap 'Mail, Contacts, Calendars'.
  3. Tap 'Add Account', then 'Other'.
  4. Select 'Add Subscribed Calendar'.
  5. Enter the calendar URL (http://www.worldcupbrazilcalendar.com/all.ics by default) in the 'Server' field.
  6. If you get a warning saying 'Cannot Connect Using SSL', tap 'Continue'.
  7. Tap 'Save'. Your calendar should now be added to your device.

Microsoft Outlook

You should be able to subscribe by clicking 'Subscribe in Desktop / Mobile Calendar' on our homepage. If not, please follow the instructions on the Microsoft website for subscribing to internet calendars and enter the calendar URL (http://www.worldcupbrazilcalendar.com/all.ics by default).


Windows Live Calendar

  1. Visit Windows Live Calendar at http://calendar.live.com.
  2. Click on 'Import' in the top toolbar.
  3. Click 'Subscribe' on the left hand side.
  4. Enter the calendar URL (http://www.worldcupbrazilcalendar.com/all.ics by default) and a name.
  5. Click 'Subscribe' and the calendar will be added.

Google Calendar

You should be able to subscribe by clicking 'Subscribe in Google Calendar' on our homepage. If not, please follow the instructions below.


  1. Go to Google Calendar and login with your Google account.
  2. Click on the small arrow next to 'Other Calendars' on the left hand side, and select 'Add by URL'.
  3. Enter the calendar URL (http://www.worldcupbrazilcalendar.com/all.ics by default) in the 'URL' field.
  4. Click 'Add Calendar'. The calendar should now be added to your Google Calendar account.


救命！我的日历没有自动更新！

本日历应该会随着赛事的进展而自动更新。


如果没更新，可能是因为你导入了日历，而非订阅。如果是这样的话，删除日历并重新根据以上教程进行订阅。


不更新也有可能是你日历程序的‘刷新率’设置得太低了。建议最长设置成每日更新一次。不能再长了！

另外谷歌日历有一个长达数年的bug，容易导致订阅的日历不自动刷新，即使重新订阅，还是显示旧版的日历。我已经在英文原版的基础上做了一点修改，试图弥补这个bug，但仍有时不能成功。如果你使用谷歌日历并且在小组赛之后发现日历没有更新，请改成使用别的日历应用程序。
