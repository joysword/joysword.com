Download (last updated 2001-10-24 with more comments from Sai, Matteo, Karl, and Evan)

Here is the zip file of the whole online book: rotk.zip (1.8 MB)
You need a zip program such as WinZip to decompress the file.

If you don't have a zip program, download this self-extract file: rotk.tmp (1.8 MB)
After you download this file, change the file name to "rotk.exe", and run it. The whole online book will be decompressed automatically.

Webmasters

You can host the .zip and .exe files in your servers for download purpose. But you need to follow a few things:

  • Use the latest-updated version of the book (available here).
  • Do not change anything.

Buy and read the book on paper: Paper books are more convenient in many occassions.

• Amazon: Three Kingdoms: A Historical Novel
Translated by Dr Moss Roberts, a professor at New York University. This is a great translation, with several maps and extensive notes and afterword, which help readers understand the novel and history in many respects. I strongly recommend this translation to the readers. However, do not buy the abridged version of this translation. (search for more)

• Amazon: Romance of the Three Kingdoms
Translated by C. H. Brewitt-Taylor. This translation is excellent in literature style. However, it uses the Yale system for translating the Chinese names. For example: Liu Pei, Ts'ao Ts'ao, and Sun Ch'üan. This system is used in many countries, but not in China. The translation of Moss Roberts and the online Romance of Three Kingdoms use the modern Pinyin system. For example: Liu Bei, Cao Cao, and Sun Quan. (search for more)

• Asiapac Books: Romance of the Three Kingdoms (comics)
Published by Asiapac Books, a set of 10 volumes has 1,500 pages of lavishly beautiful illustrations (in black & white). This comic series covers every major events of the Three Kingdoms period, from the beginning to its end.

Visit Asiac Books for other comic books related to Three Kingdoms.
